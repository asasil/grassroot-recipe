Ricewater

What is Ricewater?

Ricewater is a popular Ghanaian porridge that is prepared by using rice. It is very simple and fast and economical. Although it is generally call rice porridge, it is popular as ricewater. It got its name from the way it is prepared using lots of water. The method and ingredients for cooking is not fixed. It varies from one person to another.

ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 5 1/2 -6 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Honey/ sugar any sweetener of choice
  • Powdered or evaporated milk to serve
  • lemon grass

steps

  1. Wash rice and pour in a large saucepan with 5 cups of water and put on high heat.
  2. Boil with lemon grass for about 20 minutes till the rice has soaked the water and is soft. The lemon grass will also desolve in it giving it a very fragrance smell.
  3. Add the salt and the other cup of water if needed.
  4. Using a ladle, beat the rice against the saucepan to form a smooth- pudding-like consistency.
  5. Add the sweetener of your choice and let it simmer for about five minutes.
  6. Serve with milk of your choice. You can add groundnut if you want to.
